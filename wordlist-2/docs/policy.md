## Draft Policy For Word Inclusion

*Work in progress.*

### Open and Hyphenated Compounds

The original version of SCOWL filtered out open and hyphenated compounds.  The
new version includes most compound words found in the source lists, and are
unlikely to be removed.

However, adding new compounds that only exist in these forms is a very low
priority unless:

  1. The closed form is used, in which case the other forms will be added for
     completeness, and all will be marked as variants of each other

  2. One part of the word is not normally in the dictionary.

Extremely high-frequency word fragments that only exist as part of an open or
hyphenated compound are also candidates for inclusion on their own, but are
given a special POS of `wp` or `we` for word-part and word-ending.
